Organic vs Inorganic Food

Organic Food (Cow Dung)

  • Uses natural cow dung as a fertilizer.
  • No harmful chemicals or pesticides.
  • Rich in nutrients and antioxidants.
  • Environmentally friendly.
  • Sustainable farming practices.
Learn More

Inorganic Food (Chemical Fertilizers)

  • Relies on chemical fertilizers and pesticides.
  • Potential harmful residues.
  • Nutrient content may be lower.
  • Environmental impact due to chemical runoff.
  • May lead to soil degradation.

About the Use of Good Nutrients for Good Health

Good nutrition is essential for maintaining good health and overall well-being. Consuming a balanced diet that includes nutrient-rich foods like organic produce can provide a wide range of essential vitamins, minerals, and antioxidants that support the body's functions. It is important to prioritize the consumption of fresh and natural foods, as they can help in preventing various health issues and maintaining a strong immune system.

Difference Organic Food Inorganic Food

Antibiotics and Hormones Prohibits routine use of antibiotics May use antibiotics and hormones
